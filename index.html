NewOrbit 🇬🇧

$18.5M
Series A
VLEO Satellite Manufacturing Dual-use
UK-based NewOrbit raised $18.5 million in a Series A round announced today to commercialise Very Low Earth Orbit - the 150-450km altitude band below conventional LEO. VLEO offers significantly better imaging resolution and lower latency, but has historically been too difficult to operate due to atmospheric drag. NewOrbit's satellite platform is engineered to sustain orbit at these altitudes, opening up a new segment of near-Earth space for commercial and defence intelligence applications.
Space-Comm Outreach Angle
NewOrbit is a UK company opening up a genuinely new orbital regime - exactly the kind of home-grown innovation that plays well in front of Space-Comm's UK government and MoD buyer audience.
